About Me

We can make it together

I am a Product Designer with over 5 years of experience in UX/UI, motion, visual, and graphic design, as well as product strategy. I design mobile and web applications, AI tools, chatbots, and smartwatch experiences for transportation, logistics, eCommerce, and retail.

I lead the entire design process, from research to implementation, ensuring user-centric solutions.

As a mentor, I enjoy sharing knowledge and guiding others in their design journey. Inspired by nature, music, and true crime stories.
