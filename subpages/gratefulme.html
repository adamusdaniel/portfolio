Project Description

The goal of this project was to redesign BezKartek.com with a focus on creating a minimalist and user-friendly interface. The challenge was to achieve a clean, simple design using a black-and-white color palette while ensuring the website remained visually appealing and functional.

Key Tasks Included:
  • Emphasis on Book and Audiobook Covers: The design prioritizes showcasing the covers of books and audiobooks to make them the central visual element, enhancing the browsing experience and enabling users to easily find titles of interest.
  • Improved Product Preview and Purchase Flow: Optimized product previews for clearer, more detailed views. Streamlined the purchasing process for a more intuitive and faster user experience.
  • SEO Optimization: Ensured the minimalist design did not negatively impact search engine visibility by maintaining strong SEO elements such as metadata, product descriptions, and structured content.

The final result is a visually striking and functional design that makes browsing, discovering, and purchasing e-books and audiobooks an effortless experience, all while maintaining strong SEO and a clear, professional look.
