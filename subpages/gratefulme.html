GratefulMe – Daily Dose of Gratitude

Project Description

A simple yet powerful gratitude journal app designed to help users build a habit of positive thinking through daily reflections, gamification, and CBT-based techniques. By encouraging consistency and self-awareness, the app enhances mental well-being and fosters a more positive outlook on life.

App Goal

The app aims to help users build a gratitude habit using Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) techniques, promoting positive thinking and mental well-being through daily reflections and structured journaling.

Key Features
  • Guided Gratitude Entries – Daily prompts to encourage reflection and consistent journaling.
  • Gamification & Tracking – Streak system, achievement stats, and insights to boost motivation.
  • CBT-Based Approach – Structured gratitude practice to help shift negative thought patterns.
  • Personalized Experience – Customizable reminders, themes, and organization tools.
  • Offline-First & Private – Data stored securely on the device with no external sharing.
User Value

By making gratitude a simple, engaging, and rewarding practice, the app helps users reduce stress, improve mood, and gain a more positive perspective on life, all while strengthening their emotional resilience.

Key Tasks Included
  • Research & Concept Development – Analyze user needs, competitor apps, and define user personas to shape core user journeys.
  • Information Architecture & User Flow – Design intuitive navigation, seamless journaling, and an efficient tagging system.
  • UI Design & Visual Identity – Create a clean, calming interface with light/dark mode and a consistent design system.
  • Gamification & Engagement – Implement streak tracking, achievements, and reminders to boost motivation.
  • Onboarding & Retention – Design a smooth onboarding, non-intrusive paywall, and optimized push notifications.
Areas for Development
  • Enhanced Analytics and Gamification – Monthly statistics, progress charts, positive emotion visualization, and a challenge system to support the gratitude habit.
  • Interactive Entry Formats – Adding images, drawings, and an option to reflect on difficult moments to help break negative thinking patterns.
  • Social Features – Sharing entries, gratitude letters, and optional group challenges to strengthen interpersonal connections.
  • Integrations and Extensions – Exporting entries, lock screen widgets, and potential future synchronization with other platforms.
